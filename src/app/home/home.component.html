About Us
Our communities are self-organizing. We believe that you should have the authority to negotiate your value. We understand the need and necessity for mobility. We all must leave our homes to go to work, to school, to meetup with friends, to party or even to visit and spend time with loved ones and family. When it comes to this mobility two thought processes guides our decision – what it costs us, and our safety. But should anyone decide the price of that mobility, or can we value what that trip to the doctor’s office should cost us? Rlye was birth with a sole mission at its core – Our communities are self-organizing, with responsible respect to the value of services we provide and receive. Therefore, it should be up to that community to dictate and negotiate the value of it services. Our platform is purposed to do the following: 1. Provide a safe and reliable marketplace for ridesharing services, 2. Bring both the driver and the rider at the decision table, 3. Empower you to be the primary decision maker to how you spend or earn your money.
